Hi! My name is Sarah Ann Geren, but everybody calls me Annie (well, I'm not actually Annie, I'm her website-wizard boyfriend. She has no power here, so I can say whatever I want). Fun fact, this font was created using my real handwriting!

If there are two things people know about me, it's that I love art and I'm destined to become a crazy cat lady (or, honestly, a crazy spider lady terrifying her poor boyfriend). There's a certain joy that animals bring to our lives, and it's not something that can really be explained with words. Instead, I choose to draw people's beloved pets for them so that, no matter the future, they always have something to remember. Though their flame burns quick, it is searing hot, and the unconditional love a pet holds is something to be cherished forever.

Thank you for taking the time to visit my site!
